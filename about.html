About our firm

Big firm experience. Small firm attention.

The Stewart Law Practice, PC is a boutique firm practicing primarily in Estate Planning, Probate, Elder Law and Social Security. We combine modern technology with good old-fashioned hard work to give clients a quality, efficient experience — on their terms.

We know not every client can make it into the office. So we operate in ways that minimize in-office time while maximizing the service you receive: virtual signings, remote consultations, and home visits when appropriate. Based primarily in the North Fulton area, our goal is simple: meet your needs with probate and estate planning in the easiest, most comfortable manner possible.

While we serve anyone who retains us, our primary clients are seniors, families, and caregivers — people whose legal needs sit at the intersection of our chosen practice areas. Our founding attorney is a former financial services consultant, banker, trust officer, and entrepreneur. That hybrid background is exactly what makes her uniquely prepared to guide you through legal needs that touch your finances, your family, and your future.

What we believe

The values behind every engagement.

Compassion First

You're going through something. We meet that with patience, not paperwork. Every client gets the time they need to ask questions and understand their options.

Clarity Always

No mystery bills. No jargon. We tell you the cost, the timeline, and the options before you commit — and we explain every clause in plain English.

Family-First Counsel

We've sat where you sit. As a caregiver to her grandmother and now her mother, Deborah brings that perspective to every plan she drafts.

Quietly Efficient

We use modern technology — secure portals, e-signing, virtual meetings — to save you trips, time, and stress, while keeping every document precise.
